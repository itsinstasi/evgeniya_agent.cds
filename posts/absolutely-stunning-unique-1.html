Absolutely stunning unique villa located within the super exclusive La Zagaleta. Constructed over three levels plus an enormous sub basement area, this superb property offers everything you could possibly wish for in a property! With south facing panoramic views over the enormous 6900m2 completely flat plot, to the Mediterranean sea and beyond.
.
The villa has many striking and individual features, and is accesses via a central staircase and is adjacent to a panoramic elevate, giving full access to all levels of the property.
.
Built size 1600 sq m, Plot 6900 sq m
.
⠀
The development is widely regarded as being the most prestigious and sought after address in Europe, offering an array of resident only facilities. This magnificent country estate of almost 900 hectares (2,200 acres) and is situated in one of the most beautiful areas of the Costa del Sol, only a few kilometres from Marbella and Puerto Banús. With two gated and fully guarded entrances the estate has 24 hour high level armed security. Most villas enjoy breath-taking views of the Mediterranean, the straits of Gibraltar, the north African coast, its own golf courses and the stunning Serrania de Ronda mountain range. Each plot is separated by 10 meters of green zone, allowing added privacy and freedom of movement for the local wildlife. A total of 420 plots make up the exclusive Country Club, over half of which already have exclusive villas built on them

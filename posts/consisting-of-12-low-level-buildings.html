Consisting of 12 low-level buildings of 2 and 3-bedroom apartments (90 properties) with landscaped gardens and communal pool areas.
Each property is south or southwest facing to make the most of the Mediterranean sun and maximise natural light within. The area is as close to nature as you can get with lakes, olive trees, an abundance of bougainvillaea and rosemary.
Finished to the highest standards with the finest materials including Arabic tiles, stone and wood, the apartments follow an open-plan layout while large floor-to-ceiling glass doors bring the outside inside. The properties also include home automation systems allowing control of blinds and climate.
Communal areas include swimming pools for adults and children, a range of areas to relax in the sun or under shade. The ground floor apartments have private gardens. All properties have a designated parking space and secure private storerooms.

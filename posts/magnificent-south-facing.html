Magnificent south facing brand new five bedroom villa in the prestigious and gated community of Los Flamingos Golf; just a ten minute drive to Puerto Banus, Estepona town centre and all amenities. This property was finished being built in 2016 and is luxurious in every aspect from the top quality materials and furniture to the stunning mature tropical gardens and private salt water infinity pool overlooking the golf course with specular sea views. The entrance to the villa brings you to the first floor with the master suite with walk in wardrobe, double Jacuzzi bath and private terrace; two guest suites and access to the garage. The ground floor has an open plan living and dining area with fireplace and ceiling to floor French doors leading to the private garden and pool area; open plan fully equipped kitchen; guest suite; guest toilet and a beautiful indoor garden... -interiors

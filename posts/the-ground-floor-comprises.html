Главная / The ground floor comprises
28 May 2019
18 0

The ground floor comprises a spacious living room, with double height wooden beamed ceilings and fireplace leading onto the covered terrace, offering spacious seating areas and a fireplace with which to enjoy outside living during the colder months; an informal sitting room with fireplace and wooden beamed ceilings; an informal dining room; a winter dining room; a fully fitted and equipped kitchen with pantry and cold chamber; a guest bedroom suite with bathtub and walk-in shower, dressing area and small balcony; a second guest bedroom suite with balcony, dressing room and exit onto the pool area; a 2 bedroom guest/staff apartment with independent access; laundry room; a third guest bedroom suite (currently used as an office) and two garages for 3 cars. The upper level features an imposing master suite with double height wooden beamed cathedral ceilings, living room, kitchenette, his and hers dressing rooms, fireplace and a south facing terrace overlooking the sea. The master bathroom is stunning, with Jacuzzi, double wash basins and separate toilet. Additionally on this level there is an office with balcony, a fantastic Spa area with gymnasium with adjoining massage room, a Turkish bath, sauna, pressure shower, heated indoor pool with jet stream and toilet and a fourth guest bedroom suite with a lovely balcony with sea views. On the lower ground level there is also a wine cellar with bar, a billiard room, and a cinema room sharing a double sided fireplace. Without doubt the best beachfront property available East of Marbella!
